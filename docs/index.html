#RiotCleanup thanks you.

#RiotCleanup was started in response to the 2011 England riots.

Over 90,000 people were involved in the cleanup operation following the riots, and we'd like to say thank you to everyone who was part of #RiotCleanup.

This website used to be home to support resources, local contacts, and cleanup events for areas that were affected by the 2011 England riots.

There were several Twitter accounts for the UK and local areas:

  • @RiotCleanup, covering all over the UK
  • @RiotCleanupBrum, covering Birmingham
  • @RiotCleanupManc, covering Manchester
  • @RiotCleanupNott, covering Nottingham
  • @RiotCleanupWolv, covering Wolverhampton
